5. **Tiebreaker**: If *Quick* and *Vigilant* are tied, a 1D20 roll decides initiative, with ties resolved until one combatant rolls higher.  
6. **Delaying Initiative**: A combatant may choose to delay their action, allowing others to act first, though this delayed initiative remains fixed for the rest of the battle.  

Each turn, characters typically perform two actions: a *Combat Action* (e.g., attacking) and a *Movement Action* (e.g., moving). Reactions, such as free attacks or defense tests, occur outside the initiative order as responses to other actions.
